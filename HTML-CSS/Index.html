About me

Hello there. My name is Jorge, or George for foreigners who find it difficult to pronounce my name. I'm 31 years old, and I would describe myself as passionate about science fiction and fantasy, but also about history My dream has always been to travel around the world and discover new cultures.

I'll be honest, I didn't start this IT journey out of passion. I grew up on the internet and playing video games, but it was never my dream to work in this field. However, with the passing of the years, I discovered that maybe this might be the way to achieve my dream. Along the way, I also realized that through this world, I could find ways to help others, even in small ways, by creating tools or solutions that make their lives a bit easier.

And through this process, I found that I could mix my passions with this world and, eventually, I fell in love with it too.
